OUR DOCTORS

General Practitioner

A General Practitioner (GP), also known as a Family Physician or Primary Care Physician, is a medical doctor who provides primary healthcare services to individuals and families.

A Specialist Physician

Specialist physicians have expertise
in specific medical fields, focusing on in-depth diagnosis, management, and treatment of complex medical conditions within their specialty.

Surgeon

Surgeons are medical professionals specialized in performing surgical procedures, including pre-operative evaluations, surgery, and post-operative care..
