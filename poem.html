The Tapestry of Life
By Christian Maverick Z. Roldan

In the grand tapestry of true life,
Threads of joy and threads of strife.
Each moment a stitch, a story told,
A journey in which our souls unfold.

Sunrise whispers of new beginnings,
Embracing hope, as the day is winning.
Through valleys low and mountains high,
We traverse the canvas of the sky.

Raindrops fall, a cleansing grace,
Tears and laughter, life's embrace.
Seasons change, a constant flow,
Nature's rhythm in the ebb and flow.

In the tapestry of moments we weave,
Love persists, the strongest reprieve.
Challenges faced, lessons learned,
The symphony of life, where hearts are earned.
